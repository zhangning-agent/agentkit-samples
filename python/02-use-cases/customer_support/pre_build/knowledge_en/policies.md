## Return & Exchange Policy

- **7-Day No-Reason Return/Exchange**:
  - Eligibility: Within 7 days from receipt, item is unused, intact, and complete with accessories.
  - Process: Online request → support review → pickup or in-store handling → refund or exchange.
- **30-Day No-Reason Return/Exchange**:
  - Eligibility: Within 30 days from receipt for specific items (e.g., phones) with original packaging retained.
  - Exclusions: Customized items, activated software products, and other non-returnable goods.
- **Shipping**: Return shipping is borne by the customer unless the item has a verified quality issue.

## Warranty Policy

- **Standard Warranty Period**:
  - 180 days (~6 months), 1 year, 2 years, 3 years, up to 5 years depending on product type.
  - Start date: From purchase date.
  - Coverage: Manufacturing defects and hardware failures (non-user damage).
- **Extended Warranty**:
  - Customers can purchase an extension up to 10 years total.
  - Conditions: Must be purchased within the standard warranty period; fee depends on product value.
  - Process: Record extension in CRM and update the warranty end date.
- **Warranty Exclusions**:
  - User damage (water damage, drops), unauthorized modifications, and normal wear.
