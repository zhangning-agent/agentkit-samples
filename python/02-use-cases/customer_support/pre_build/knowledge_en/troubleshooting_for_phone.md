## Phone Troubleshooting Guide

**Purpose**: Help support agents guide customers through common issues and reduce unnecessary repairs.

### Common Issues and Steps

- **Issue 1: Phone wonâ€™t power on**
  - Step 1: Check battery level and charge for 30 minutes before retrying.
  - Step 2: Try a forced restart (e.g., hold power + volume down for 10 seconds).
  - Step 3: If still unresponsive, it may be a hardware issue; recommend service.
- **Issue 2: Screen is blank or touch is unresponsive**
  - Step 1: Restart the phone and check for software conflicts.
  - Step 2: Clean the screen and remove any screen protector for testing.
  - Step 3: If the issue persists, the screen may be damaged; replace the component.
- **Issue 3: Battery drains too fast**
  - Step 1: Check background apps and close unnecessary ones.
  - Step 2: Lower brightness and disable Bluetooth/GPS to save power.
  - Step 3: If the battery is aged, recommend replacement (free within warranty).
- **Issue 4: Network connection problems**
  - Step 1: Toggle airplane mode on and off to re-search the network.
  - Step 2: Check the SIM card seating and test in another device.
  - Step 3: Update system software or reset network settings.
- **Issue 5: Camera cannot focus**
  - Step 1: Clean the lens to remove smudges.
  - Step 2: Restart the camera app or update camera software.
  - Step 3: If hardware failure is suspected, request service within warranty.
